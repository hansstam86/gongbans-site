The hardware that quietly powers everything.

A gongban (公板, "public board") is a Chinese factory reference design — a working PCB you can copy, brand, and ship. Most consumer electronics from China are built on a small library of these. Here's the start of that library, with sourcing notes from twenty years inside the supply chain.
