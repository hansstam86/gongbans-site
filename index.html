# Gumroad Product Description for GONGBANS --- ## Short Description (for the subtitle/tagline field) The practical guide to building hardware products faster, cheaper, and with less risk — using the shared circuit board system that already powers half the world's consumer electronics. --- ## Full Description There is a product sitting on your desk right now — a smartwatch, a wireless earbud case, a Bluetooth speaker, a GPS tracker. Whatever brand is printed on the box, there is a very good chance that the circuit board inside it was not designed by the company that sold it to you. It was probably a gongban. **Gongban (公板) means "public board."** It's a fully designed, fully tested, production-ready printed circuit board — available for free or near-free — to any manufacturer, brand, or entrepreneur who wants to build a product around it. Right now, thousands of products are being manufactured in Shenzhen on gongban platforms. Some are sold by brands you've never heard of. Some are sold by brands you use every day. The board underneath is the same — the difference is in the firmware, the casing, the app, and the brand story built on top. **Most Western hardware startups don't know this system exists.** They spend $200K–$1M and 12–24 months designing custom circuit boards from scratch. Over 70% fail before their first production run — usually because they ran out of money during development. This book changes that. --- ### What you'll learn - What gongbans are, how they work at a technical level, and where they come from - How to find and evaluate the right gongban for your product idea - The solution house ecosystem — who builds these boards and how to work with them - Gongmo (public molds) — the matching ecosystem for product casings and enclosures - How to customize firmware on a reference platform you didn't design - Building companion apps and cloud backends for gongban-based products - The real economics — what things cost, where the margins are, what to watch out for - Regulatory certification using pre-certified modules (FCC, CE, and beyond) - Supply chain management, factory relationships, and quality control - How to negotiate, structure contracts, and protect yourself legally - When to graduate from gongban to custom hardware — and how to make that transition - Every major pitfall and red flag, with real examples of what goes wrong --- ### What's inside 20 chapters and 10+ deep-dive addendums covering the full journey from idea to mass production. No fluff. No theory without practice. Every chapter includes real numbers, real timelines, and real contacts. **Plus a complete appendix** with solution house directories, sourcing tools, certification resources, a requirements document template, and recommended reading. --- ### Who this is for - **Hardware entrepreneurs** who want to get a connected device to market in months, not years - **Product managers** building hardware product lines who need to understand the supply chain options their competitors are already using - **Startup founders and investors** who need to evaluate hardware feasibility, timelines, and budgets with real information --- ### The bottom line The gongban system is not a secret in Shenzhen. It's the way things work. The only secret is that most Western entrepreneurs don't know about it. **Custom PCB path:** $200K–$1M+, 12–24 months, 70%+ failure rate. **Gongban path:** $50K–$200K, 3–6 months, building on designs already proven by hundreds of manufacturers. Stop building from zero. Ship it.
